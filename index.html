🏪 Build on a Slot

Shops AND street furniture share the same plots — choose wisely. Order matters: fast/cheap shops early, benches to slow crowds down before pricier shops. 🍽️ Chaat Corner holds a big crowd at once — great for absorbing a bus-load before they spread out.

🎭 How Street Furniture Works

🪑 Bench — customers actually sit down for a few seconds. Free, but counts toward happiness.
📰 Newsstand — customers stop to read, pay a small ₹ amount, counts toward happiness.
🎶 Music Ads — customers dance as they walk past. No stop, just joy (and a happiness tick).
🚌 Bus Stop — an actual bus drives in on a timer, drops off 3 customers right there, then leaves.

Get 100% of today's customers happy (served by any shop, bench, or newsstand) before the day ends for a big bonus.
